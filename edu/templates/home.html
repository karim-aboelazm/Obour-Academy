Our Message

The Obour Higher Institute of Engineering and Technology in Obour seeks, through its architectural, electrical and civil programs, to the integrated development of the student's personality, and to build the professional ingredients for a distinguished engineer who keeps pace with developments in the labor market, capable of creativity, continuous learning, production and development of applied scientific research to serve society and develop the environment.

Our Vision

That the Higher Transit Institute be among the top ten Egyptian higher education institutions during the next decade, and actively contribute to achieving the goals of sustainable development in the Egyptian society and occupy a prominent position among the colleges and higher institutes for the study of engineering sciences in Egypt and supply the labor market with a distinguished graduate and provide an advanced administrative and academic work environment for teaching and learning Scientific research, community service and development
